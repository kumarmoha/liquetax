üöÄ Step-by-Step Registration Process

Step 1: Choose Business Structure

Evaluate your business needs and select the most suitable structure based on liability, funding requirements, and growth plans.

Step 2: Name Approval

Check name availability and apply for name reservation through the MCA portal or relevant authority.

Step 3: Obtain DIN & DSC

Apply for Director Identification Number and Digital Signature Certificate for all directors.

Step 4: File Incorporation

Submit incorporation application with required documents and fees to the registrar.
